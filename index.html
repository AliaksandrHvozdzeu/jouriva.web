Travel Route Video Creator

Turn your journey into a stylish animated story

Jouriva is a fun travel video creator that helps you build a route, choose transport, watch movement across the map, and export a ready-to-post video for social media.

What You Can Do

Build Any Route

Add a start point, stops, and final destination in a simple route builder.

Choose Transport

Pick how you travel: car, bus, plane, or ship.

Live Movement on Map

Watch your vehicle move along the route in real time.

Video Customization & Export

Tune speed, vehicle size, aspect ratio, and map details, then export straight to your device gallery.

Distance Display

Show distance in km, mi, or hide it when you want a cleaner look.

Border-Aware Visuals

Country flags update automatically as your route crosses borders.
